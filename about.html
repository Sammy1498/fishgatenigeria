Our Story

We didn't start with a business plan. We started with a pond.

From a single experiment in water chemistry to Nigeria's most trusted aquaculture consultancy — this is a story written in data, discipline, and an obsession with getting the variables right.

Seven years of questioning everything the textbooks said.

Most people enter aquaculture looking for a shortcut. We entered it looking for the truth. What began as a personal quest to understand why fish die in seemingly healthy water became a decade-long documentation project — testing every variable, recording every failure, and building protocols that actually work in African conditions.

Today, that obsessive documentation is the foundation of everything we do: from the fingerlings we breed, to the hatcheries we design, to the policy frameworks we advocate for.

2017

The First Pond

Started with a single experimental pond in Abeokuta, testing water chemistry variables that nobody else was documenting.

2019

From Failure to Formula

After two years of systematic testing, we developed our first reproducible breeding protocol with documented survival rates above 85%.

2021

Scaling the Science

Expanded from personal research to commercial consultancy — helping 50+ farms adopt data-driven practices across Northern Nigeria.

2023

The Fishgate Standard

Launched our training academy and began partnering with government bodies on aquaculture policy reform and community empowerment.

Now

Legacy in the Making

Breeding premium stock, designing luxury aquariums, training the next generation, and setting the standard for African aquaculture.

Our Mission

To make precision aquaculture the standard, not the exception, across Africa.

We combine seven years of documented research with hands-on field expertise to deliver fish stocks, hatchery designs, and training programmes that don't just work — they set the benchmark.

Our Vision

A continent where every fish farm is built on science, not guesswork.

We envision a future where data-driven aquaculture is accessible to every farmer — from the commercial hatchery operator to the community cooperative — creating sustainable livelihoods and food security across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Promise

We don't sell hope. We sell protocols that are proven in the water.

Every recommendation we make is backed by documented trial data. Every stock we sell is bred under strict biological standards. Every system we build is engineered to last. That's the Fishgate guarantee.
