Common questions, straight answers.

What's the minimum order for fingerlings?

Our minimum order starts at 1,000 fingerlings for commercial clients. For smaller projects or ornamental species, we're flexible — reach out and we'll find the right fit.

Do you ship outside Nasarawa State?

Yes. We deliver across Nigeria with specialized live-transport systems that maintain water quality and temperature throughout the journey. Survival rates exceed 95%.

How long does a hatchery consultation take?

An initial site assessment takes 1–2 days. Full design and build-out timelines depend on scale, but most projects are operational within 4–8 weeks.

Can I visit the farm before committing?

Absolutely. We encourage farm visits for serious clients. Book an appointment through this form or call us directly, and we'll arrange a guided tour of our facilities.

Ready to build a legacy that breathes?

We only accept a limited number of specialized projects and consultations each season to ensure every ecosystem receives our full technical precision. Whether you are starting a commercial farm or a private gallery, let's ensure your first step is the right one.
